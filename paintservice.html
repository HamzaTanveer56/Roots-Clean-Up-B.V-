Roots Clean Up B.V in short

Roots Clean Up B.V was founded in 2021. Roots Clean Up has developed into a professional cleaning company with more than 500 employees, branches in Bergschenhoek and Woerden and a complete package of cleaning services.

Our goal is to grow into a Top 20 cleaning company in a fun way, while maintaining our regional character. We aim for an 8+ experience with both our clients and employees. That is why we aim for excellent quality, optimal customer experience.
